    about me:

    My first introduction to coding was playing the QBASIC game Gorillas.Bas, a game where two gorillas take turns trying to hit each other with bananas. My grandpa showed me how to edit the source code to make the bananas travel at the speed of light. Since then coding (and tinkering) have always been part of my life.

    I've spent the last 14 years working in the IT MSP industry. I've spent time as a field engineer, procurement specialist, manager, and currently, director. Something common in all the positions I've had is that I approach each task like I'm solving a mystery; I gather resources, anaylyze clues, and present findings to 'solve' the case. This approach has helped me grow revenue, increase profit and make departments more efficient.

    I'm always interested in solving larger, more complex problems. The challenge is part of the reward. I have found Software Engineering to be a great career in that all my efforts are always rewarded back to me.
